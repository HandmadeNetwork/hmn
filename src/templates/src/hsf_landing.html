Software is terrible.

The Handmade Software Foundation is a pending 501(c)(6) nonprofit dedicated to supporting the creation of Handmade software and promoting the Handmade ethos in the software industry.

Modern software is a disaster. Computers have become hundreds of times faster, yet they feel slower to use than they did two decades ago. Thoughtless software development practices have run the industry into the ground. But we believe there's a better way.
